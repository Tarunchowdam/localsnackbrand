Our Story

Rooted in Coffs Harbour’s banana plantations

From Local Farms to Your Table

Snackonomy began in the heart of Coffs Harbour, surrounded by lush banana plantations and a community of dedicated farmers. We saw an opportunity to create snacks that not only taste amazing but also support the growers who nurture our land.

Our mission is simple: to craft functional food snacks from local produce, empowering our community while promoting health and wellness. Every bite tells a story of sustainability, care, and collaboration.

Meet Our Growers

Our partner farmers are the heart of Snackonomy. By working directly with local growers, we ensure fair trade, quality produce, and sustainable farming practices.

Our Team

From food scientists to community advocates, our team is united by a love for healthy living and local pride. Together, we innovate snacks that are good for you and good for the region.
